Project Euler 30

Project Euler problem #30 seems much easier than say #30. Really I was imaging the problems will be harder and harder. Or it may be a chill before a storm. Anyway, problems asks us to find the sum of all the numbers which are equal to sum of their digits raised by 5th power.

In this kind of problems one thing I do the very first - search for boundaries so not to write infinite loop. 9^5 is 59049, so 5 digit number will have a limit of 295245, 6 digit - 354294, 7 digit - 413343. That’s when I knew it. 7 digit number cannot be generated by the sum of it’s digits raised by 5th. So my guess for upper lim is 354294. For lower - 2 is enough as problem clearly states that 1 is not a sum on it’s own(1 == 1^5).
